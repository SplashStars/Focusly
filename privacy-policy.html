Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

No data collected

The short version: Focusly stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on our servers.

1. Information We Collect

Focusly does NOT collect any personal information. The app operates entirely offline.

We do not collect: your name, email, location data, device identifiers, advertising IDs, usage analytics, crash reports, or any data that leaves your device.

2. How Your Data Is Stored

All app data is stored locally on your Android device using SQLite. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly back it up through Android built-in backup.

3. Third-Party Services

Focusly does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services including Google Analytics, Firebase, or Facebook SDK.

4. Notifications

Focusly sends local push notifications to remind you about tasks and habits. These are generated entirely on your device with no data sent to external servers.

5. Data Backup

If you use Android built-in backup, your app data may be backed up to your Google account. This is controlled by your Android settings and Google backup policy, not Focusly.

6. Children Privacy

Focusly is suitable for all ages and is rated Everyone on Google Play. Since we do not collect any personal information, there are no special concerns regarding children privacy.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we change this privacy policy, we will update the Last updated date at the top of this page and notify users through an app update on Google Play.

9. Your Rights

All your data is stored locally on your device. You can delete it by uninstalling Focusly or clearing app data in Android Settings.
